88 Rising | Branding Motion Identity

A motion graphic piece where we have to create a branding identity via motion animation. This requires finding and using a total of 25 footage and 6 audios. 88rising is a media music group that helps promote music artists and including dancers of Asian background. This was intended to help those who were having a hard time breaking into the entertainment industry where people of Asian background were being under represented.
